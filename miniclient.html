WMS Mini Client

This is a mini program developped in the framework of WeChat, the biggest social media in Chinese market, This mini App is developped to connect to your same WIFI hosted MOCA instance, with this phone-based App, you can do a few things:

  • View a series of reports, picking, work queue
  • View facility data, like area, location, picking zone, movement zone, work zone etc
  • Scan barcode for LPN, Inbound shipment for information query
  • LPN receiving, similar to RF pallet receiving
  • Move LPN in the warehouse
  • Outbound features in developping...

If you are interested, you can download WeChat from Apple Store, from Mini Program search 'Sam WMS'
